Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026.

What we collect

Menopause Reviewed collects only the information needed to operate the site and improve content quality.

  • Analytics data. We use Google Analytics 4 to understand which articles readers find useful. This includes anonymized IP address, browser type, country, pages visited, and time on page.
  • Newsletter subscriptions. If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address and the date you subscribed. We never sell, rent, or share email lists.
  • Email correspondence. If you email us, we retain that correspondence to respond and follow up.

What we don't collect

  • We do not collect health information, symptom data, or any personal medical history.
  • We do not require accounts or logins to read articles.
  • We do not sell, rent, or transfer your data to third parties for marketing.

Cookies & advertising

We use cookies for essential site function and analytics. We may serve display advertising via Google AdSense or similar networks. These networks may use cookies to serve ads based on your interests across the web. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.

Your rights

If you are in the EU/UK (GDPR) or California (CCPA), you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to opt out of data sales. We don't sell data, but you may still request access or deletion. Email privacy@menopausereviewed.com.

Children's privacy

Menopause Reviewed is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16.

Changes to this policy

We will update the "Last updated" date when this policy changes. Material changes will be noted on the homepage.

Contact

Questions? Email privacy@menopausereviewed.com.
